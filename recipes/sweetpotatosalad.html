Sweet Potato and Pomegranate Salad

Description

This is a nice fall salad to include with your Thanksgiving meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of arugula
  • A handful of mint leaves
  • 1 cup of (cooled) roasted sweet potato
  • 2 tablespoons of roasted pine nuts
  • 2 tablespoons of pomegranate arils
  • 2 tablespoons of feta
  • Dressing made of equal parts lemon juice, pomegranate juice, tahini, and olive oil

    • Steps

    1. Mix together dressing
    2. Mix all ingredients
