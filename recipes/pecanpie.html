Pecan Pie

Description

This is the only pecan pie I've ever made, and it's the only pecan pie I will ever make. Recipe from Tasting History.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped pecans
  • A pinch of salt
  • Pecan halves, for a layer on top of the filling

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. In a saucepan, whisk together the milk, sugar, eggs, flour, chopped pecans, and salt. Set it over medium-low heat and gently stir. Be sure to do this over low heat because we don't want the eggs to scramble. Cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture steams, and continue to cook for about 3 minutes. Adjust the heat if necesary so that the mixture only steams, not simmers. Pour into the cooled pie shell.
  3. Add a layer of pecan halves on top of the filling/li>
  4. Bake 35 minutes
