Cranberry Sauce

Description

Delicious cranberry sauce you can make ahead for Thanksgiving Day. Credit to Amanda Saab (Amanda's Plate) for the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of fresh whole cranberries
  • 3/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon orange zest
  • 1 stick of cinnamon
  • 1 nutmeg pod
  • 1 star anise pod
  • 1/2 TBSP freshly chopped mint

Steps

  1. In a heavy bottom saucepan combine ingredients
  2. Bring to a boil
  3. Reduce heat to medium low and cook for 8-10 minutes
  4. Remove spice pods and allow to cool
